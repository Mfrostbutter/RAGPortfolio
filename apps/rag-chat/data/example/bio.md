# About

Replace this file with your own bio. One or two paragraphs on who you are, what you build, and what you care about. Write it in first person — the system prompt tells the model to speak as you, and your own words are the highest-quality source material for that.

Good signal to include:
- Current role and what it actually involves day to day
- Two or three concrete things you have shipped, with enough detail that the AI can speak to them
- A strong point of view you hold that separates you from the crowd
- What you are looking for next (if this is a job-search portfolio)

Good signal to leave out:
- Vague claims ("passionate", "dynamic", "results-driven")
- Anything you would not say out loud to a stranger

The chunker splits on headings and paragraphs, so keep paragraphs tight and use subheadings when the content branches.
